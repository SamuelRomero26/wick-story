"Not bad, Wick," Deadpool said, grinning. "But I'm just getting started." Wick said nothing. He knew this fight was far from over. The battle between the two deadly fighters had only just begun. Suddenly Deadpool hopped in a car and John Wick was right behind him. Both of them were weaving in and out of traffic almost crashing multiple times but saving themselves at the last second.
